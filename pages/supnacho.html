Super Nachos

Super Nachos... What more needs to be said about this true masterpiece of a food. Truly, there is none like it.

Ingredients

  • Refried Beans
  • Sour Cream
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Jalapenos

Directions

  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Spread beans on bottom of 9x13 pan
  3. Add cooked ground beef to pan on top of beans
  4. Add sour cream and spread
  5. Add cheese
  6. Take half of your chopped tomatoes and jalapenos and sprinkle them over the cheese
  7. Bake for 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbling
