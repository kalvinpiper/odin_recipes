The Alt' Malt

The Chocolate Malt, I'm getting hungry just thinking about the sweet ice cream drink, when you find the perfect combination of Chocolate and Malt mix there is truly nothing better.

Ingredients

  • Ice Cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Malt mix
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Add 3 scoops of ice cream to a blender
  2. Add 1/2 cup milk
  3. Add 2 tablespoons Chocolate syrup
  4. Add 2 tablespoons Malt mix
  5. Blend together until mixed well
  6. Serve with a straw
