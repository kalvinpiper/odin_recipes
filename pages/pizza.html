Pizza

The Za' is far and away the best food out there, a combination of dairy, meats and Vegetables mean that this food is rivaled by none other.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Assorted Cheeses
  • Assorted Meats
  • Assorted Vegetables

Directions

  1. Lay the Dough out on a pan
  2. Flatten out that Dough
  3. Pour some sauce on that dough
  4. Add the cheeses
  5. Add the meats
  6. Add the veggies
  7. Throw that in the oven for 15 minutes
